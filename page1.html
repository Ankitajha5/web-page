  • ARTIST:

    • An artist is a person who creates art with the help of their creative abilities and ideas. Without an artist, the world will be like a tree without leaves, flowers, or fruits. Artists use their creative medium of art work to communicate their thoughts and feelings about a variety of topics, such as the current situation, the beauty of nature, society, etc., history, their perspective on the world, and so on.Throughout history, the importance of art and artists has remained constant. Artists play an important role in preserving our culture, heritage, language, and skills, as well as passing on knowledge from one generation to the next.
